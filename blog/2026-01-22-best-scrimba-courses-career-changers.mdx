# Best Scrimba Courses for Career Changers

Changing careers is scary. You don't have 4 years for a CS degree, and you might not have $15,000 for a bootcamp.

Scrimba sits in the "goldilocks" zone: structured enough to keep you on track, but flexible enough to fit around your current job. Here is the exact roadmap we recommend for career changers in 2026.

{/* truncate */}

## The "Safe Bet" Recommendation

If you are switching from a non-tech field (marketing, sales, retail, hospitality), we recommend the **[Fullstack Developer Path](/docs/paths/fullstack-developer-path)** (108.4 hrs).

**Why Fullstack?**
*   **More Job Opportunities:** You are eligible for Frontend, Backend, and Fullstack roles.
*   **Context:** Understanding how databases work makes you a better frontend developer, even if you never write backend code professionally.
*   **Safety:** It prevents you from hitting a "ceiling" early in your learning where you can build a UI but can't save data.

## The "Fast Track" Recommendation

If you need to be employed in under 5 months, switch to the **[Frontend Developer Path](/docs/paths/frontend-developer-path)** (81.6 hrs).

It cuts out the backend complexity (Node/SQL) so you can focus 100% on getting really, really good at React. This is the fastest route to a paycheck, but it limits your initial job pool to purely frontend roles.

## The 6-Month "Career Changer" Roadmap

Assuming you work full-time and can study **10-15 hours per week**:

### Month 1: The Basics (HTML/CSS)
*   **Focus:** [Learn HTML & CSS](/docs/courses/) (Free)
*   **Goal:** Build a simple personal website.
*   **Reality Check:** If you hate this month, pause. Web development might not be for you.

### Month 2: JavaScript (The Hard Part)
*   **Focus:** JavaScript Modules in the career path.
*   **Goal:** Build a Blackjack game or Calculator.
*   **Tip:** Do not rush. This is where 50% of people quit. It *is* hard. That is normal.

### Month 3: React (The "Aha!" Moment)
*   **Focus:** [Learn React](/docs/courses/react/)
*   **Goal:** Build the "Tenzies" game and a "Movie Search" app.
*   **Milestone:** You are now capable of building modern web apps.

### Month 4: Advanced Skills & Portfolio
*   **Focus:** APIs, Git, and Responsive Design.
*   **Goal:** Build your **Magnum Opus** project. This should be a unique idea (e.g., "A budgeting app for dog walkers"), not a tutorial clone.

### Month 5: The "Get Hired" Push
*   **Focus:** The "Getting Hired" module (Resume, LinkedIn).
*   **Goal:** Apply to 20 jobs. Get rejected by 20 jobs. Refine resume. Repeat.

### Month 6: Interview Prep & Polish
*   **Focus:** [JavaScript Interview Challenges](/docs/courses/javascript/javascript-interview-challenges).
*   **Goal:** Land your first phone screens.
