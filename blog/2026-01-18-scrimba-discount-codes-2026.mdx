# Scrimba Discount Codes & Deals 2026

**Quick Answer:** Scrimba does not offer public coupon codes. The three legitimate ways to save are: (1) annual billing (typically 40–50% savings vs monthly), (2) student discount via academic email or GitHub Student Pack (1 free month), and (3) location-based PPP pricing for lower-cost regions. Don't use a VPN to spoof location — it can result in a ban.

*Last reviewed: March 2026. Discount terms and percentages may change — verify with Scrimba or their help centre.*

## Who This Is For

Anyone looking to reduce Scrimba Pro cost: students, learners committed to 3+ months, or people in regions with automatic PPP pricing.

{/* truncate */}

## 1. The "Semester" or "Annual" Discount

This is the standard discount available to everyone. By default, the monthly price is higher. If you switch to **Annual Billing**, you typically save around **40-50%** compared to paying month-to-month.

*   **Who it's for:** Anyone committed to learning for more than 3 months.
*   **How to claim:** Simply select "Yearly" or "Semester" toggle on the pricing page.

## 2. Student Discount (Valid Student ID)

Scrimba offers a substantial discount for students enrolled in a university or college.

*   **Who it's for:** Full-time or part-time students with a valid academic email address (`.edu` etc.) or proof of enrollment.
*   **How to claim:**
    1. Create a free Scrimba account.
    2. Email **help@scrimba.com** from your student email address.
    3. Subject line: "Student Discount Request".
    4. Ask politely for the student discount link.

## 3. GitHub Student Developer Pack

If you have the [GitHub Student Developer Pack](https://education.github.com/pack), you get **1 free month of Scrimba Pro**.

*   **Who it's for:** Any student verified by GitHub.
*   **What you get:** 1 month of free Pro — full access to all courses and Discord. After that, you can subscribe at the regular rate.
*   **How to claim:** Go to the [GitHub Education page](https://education.github.com/pack/offers) and search for Scrimba.

## 4. Purchasing Power Parity (PPP)

Scrimba automatically applies regional pricing discounts based on where you live. If you are in a country with lower purchasing power (e.g., India, Brazil, Nigeria), you will automatically see a significantly lower price than someone in the US or UK.

*   **IMPORTANT:** Do not use a VPN. If you use a VPN to spoof your location, Scrimba's fraud detection will likely ban your account. The discount applies to your *actual* location.

## Choose Your Discount

**Choose annual billing if:** You're committed to learning for 3+ months and want the largest savings (40–50% vs monthly).

**Choose the student discount if:** You have a valid academic email or GitHub Student Developer Pack eligibility.

**Check PPP pricing if:** You're in a region where Scrimba applies automatic location-based discounts — no code needed.
