# Scrimba for Teams: Enterprise Review

Most corporate training platforms are boring. They offer 40-hour video playlists that employees play at 2x speed while checking email.

Scrimba is different for one key reason: **It is active, not passive.**

If you are a CTO, Engineering Manager, or L&D Lead, here is why Scrimba for Teams might be your best ROI for 2026.

## The "Manager" Dashboard

The key difference between a personal Pro account and a Teams account is visibility. The Manager Dashboard allows you to:

1.  **Assign Seats:** Easily add/remove members. If an intern leaves, you can reassign their seat to a new hire.
2.  **Track Progress:** See exactly who is learning what.
    *   *Did the new junior dev finish the React module yet?*
    *   *Who is actively taking the new AI Engineering course?*
3.  **Bill Consolidating:** One invoice for the whole company, rather than expensing 10 separate credit card charges.

## Top 3 Corporate Use Cases

### 1. The "Bootcamp-in-a-Box" Onboarding
Instead of paying seniors to teach juniors standard React patterns, assign new hires the **Frontend Developer Path**.
*   **Benefit:** They learn the same standardized, MDN-approved curriculum. Your seniors spend less time explaining `useEffect` and more time explaining your specific codebase.

### 2. The AI Transformation
Every company wants to integrate AI, but few developers know how to build with LLMs.
*   **Solution:** Assign the **AI Engineer Path** to your senior devs. IN one weekend (11.4 hours), they will understand RAG, Agents, and the Vercel AI SDK.

### 3. "Use It or Lose It" L&D Budgets
Many companies have a learning stipend. Scrimba is a low-friction way to provide high-quality training that developers actually want to use.
