<DisclosureNotice />

**Quick answer:** Scrimba Pro is worth paying for if you will use structured paths consistently for 3+ months. If you only need a quick preview or sporadic learning, stay on free until your study cadence is stable.

## What actually changes with Pro

Pro unlocks full career paths, broader course access, and deeper progression routes. Free is excellent for validating fit, but it is usually not enough for full path completion alone.

## ROI framing that avoids hype

Use this decision logic:

1. Estimate weekly time you can sustain for 12 weeks
2. Define project outcomes you must ship
3. Compare Pro cost vs expected acceleration and accountability

If you cannot commit consistent time, a paid plan will not fix that by itself.
