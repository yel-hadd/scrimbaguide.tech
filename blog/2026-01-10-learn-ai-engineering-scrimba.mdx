# How to Learn AI Engineering on Scrimba

<DisclosureNotice />

Follow the AI Engineer Path (11.4h, Pro) or pick individual courses. Covers agents, RAG, MCP, Vercel AI SDK. 12 AI courses.

## Who This Is For

Web developers adding AI skills: those wanting agents, RAG, or MCP; or anyone choosing between the path vs à la carte courses.

AI Engineering is the fastest-growing specialization in web development. It's not about training foundation models or doing heavy math; it's about **engineering applications** that use LLMs. Scrimba has one of the most comprehensive AI engineering curricula available, **12 courses** covering everything from the OpenAI API to cutting-edge topics like Model Context Protocol (MCP) and RAG.

## Two Approaches to Learning AI

You can either follow the curated **Career Path** or take **individual courses** à la carte.

### Option A: The AI Engineer Path (Structured, Pro)

The [AI Engineer Path](/docs/paths/ai-engineer-path/) is an 11.4-hour structured curriculum designed to take you from a standard web developer to an AI Engineer.

**Curriculum Sequence:**

1.  **Intro to AI Engineering** (2.4 hrs): Basics of LLMs, prompts, and the OpenAI SDK.
2.  **Deployment** (52 min): How to ship AI apps to production.
3.  **Embeddings & Vector dbs** (95 min): The foundation of "memory" for AI.
4.  **AI Agents** (117 min): Building autonomous agents that can use tools and loop.
5.  **Context Engineering** (58 min): Optimizing what you feed the model.
6.  **Vercel AI SDK** (113 min): Using the industry-standard SDK for React AI apps.
7.  **Model Context Protocol** (37 min): The new standard for connecting AI to data.

### Option B: The "Free-First" Strategy

If you're not ready for a Pro subscription, Scrimba offers a surprising amount of high-quality AI content for free. If budget is your main constraint, compare routes by cost in our breakdown of the [cheapest way to learn AI agents in 2026](/blog/cheapest-way-to-learn-ai-agents-2026/).

**Step 1: The Basics (Free)**

- **[Learn RAG](/docs/courses/ai/rag/)**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation is essential for building chatbots that know your own data.

## What You Need to Know Before Starting

You definitely **do not** need to know Python. That's the beauty of Scrimba's path, it is 100% JavaScript and TypeScript.

**Prerequisites:**
- **JavaScript**: Intermediate level (Promises, async/await, objects).
- **React**: Basics are helpful for the UI parts (Vercel AI SDK), but server-side Node.js logic is the core.
- **API Knowledge**: Understanding how to make fetch requests and handle JSON.

## Why Scrimba for AI?

Most AI tutorials are just watching videos of Python boilerplate code. Scrimba is different:

1.  **It's all JavaScript**: If you're a web dev, you don't need to learn a new language.
2.  **Browser-based**: You don't need to mess with Python environments, pip, or conda. It just works.
3.  **Real Projects**: You build support agents, document Q&A bots, and semantic search engines, not just "Hello World".
