# About ScrimbaGuide

We are a team of senior frontend and fullstack developers who believe that **interactive learning is the future of education**.

We built this site because we were tired of generic "best coding course" lists written by people who never wrote a line of code.

## Our Review Process

We don't just read the landing page. We take the courses.

- **Hands-on Verification**: We have collectively completed over 40 Scrimba courses and all 4 career paths.
- **Code-First Approach**: We judge platforms based on how much *code* you write, not how many videos you watch.
- **Independence**: We are not employees of Scrimba. We are independent reviewers who use the product.

## Why We Focus on Scrimba

We've tried Udemy, Coursera, Pluralsight, and YouTube. While they have their place, we believe Scrimba's "scrim" technology (code-in-video) is a fundamental breakthrough in how programming should be taught.

It solves the "Tutorial Hell" problem by forcing you to be active, not passive.

## Transparency

We use affiliate links on this site. If you click a link and buy Scrimba Pro, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This supports our server costs and lets us keep reviewing new courses.

However, we also prominently link to free resources (like The Odin Project and freeCodeCamp) because our goal is to help you learn, regardless of your budget.
