# About ScrimbAGuide

**ScrimbAGuide** is the unofficial, comprehensive companion for students learning to code on Scrimba.

## Our Mission

Scrimba is one of the best platforms for learning web development, but with 80+ courses and multiple career paths, it can be hard to know where to start.

Our mission is to help you:
1.  **Find the perfect course** for your skill level and goals.
2.  **Save money** by explaining the pricing model and finding valid discounts.
3.  **Get hired** by providing roadmaps and career advice.

## Who We Are

We are a small team of senior web developers and Scrimba enthusiasts. We've used Scrimba to learn React, Vue, AI engineering, and more. We believe in the "interactive video" format and want to help more people discover it.

## Editorial Standards

All reviews on this site are written by real developers who have actually taken the courses. We value honesty and transparency. If a course is outdated or not worth your time, we will tell you.

*Disclaimer: We are not employees of Scrimba. This site is a fan project.*
