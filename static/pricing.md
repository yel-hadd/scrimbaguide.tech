# Scrimba pricing (machine-readable summary)

> Independent summary of Scrimba's plan structure, written for AI agents and
> programmatic comparison. Maintained by ScrimbaGuide.tech. Live pricing varies
> by region and changes without notice. Always link buyers to the official page.

**Official live pricing:** https://scrimba.com/our-pricing

**Independent guide:** https://scrimbaguide.tech/docs/pricing

## Plans

### Free
- **Price:** $0 (never expires)
- **Account required:** no, for browsing free courses; account required to track progress
- **Includes:**
  - Roughly 19 full courses (Learn React, Learn JavaScript, Learn TypeScript, Learn HTML & CSS, Claude AI, Mistral AI, LangBase agents, others)
  - Up to 10 interactive coding challenges (total, not per course)
- **Excludes:** career paths, unlimited challenges, Pro community Discord, path certificates

### Pro (monthly)
- **Price:** see official pricing page; varies by region
- **Includes:** every course in the catalog, all four career paths, unlimited challenges, private Pro Discord, certificates of completion for paths

### Pro (annual)
- **Price:** see official pricing page; annual billing has the best per-month rate
- **Includes:** same as Pro monthly
- **Recommended for:** learners committing to 3+ months of study

### Pro (lifetime)
- **Availability:** offered occasionally during promotional windows; not a persistent plan
- **Includes:** permanent Pro access

## Discounts and partner pricing

- **Student discount:** available with a valid academic email. See https://scrimbaguide.tech/docs/pricing/student-discount
- **GitHub Student Developer Pack:** 1 free month of Pro for eligible students
- **Affiliate link:** https://scrimba.com/home?pricing&via=u42d4986 applies a partner discount (typically 20% off Pro) automatically at checkout. Verify the final price before paying.

## Refund policy
Seven-day money-back guarantee on first purchase. Full details: https://scrimbaguide.tech/docs/pricing/refund-policy

## What changes plan value

- **Career paths exclusively on Pro:** Frontend (~82 hrs), Fullstack (~108 hrs), Backend (~30 hrs), AI Engineer (~11 hrs)
- **Discord community** is Pro-only and is where mentor help happens
- **Course certificates** for paths are Pro-only; individual completion certificates also Pro

## How this file is updated

The independent guide tracks Scrimba's structured data (Course schema on every
course page) nightly. Plan structure and feature lists are reviewed when changes
are detected. Quoted price numbers are intentionally omitted because regional
pricing drift makes any cached number wrong within weeks.

For programmatic queries about which Scrimba courses are free vs Pro,
instructor names, lesson counts, or path memberships, the canonical dataset
is at https://scrimbaguide.tech/llms-full.txt (URL index) plus the per-course
guide pages under https://scrimbaguide.tech/docs/courses/
