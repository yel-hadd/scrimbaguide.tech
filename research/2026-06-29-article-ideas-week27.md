- Sentence-case headings only. No Title Case, no emojis, no curly quotes.
- All internal links end with a trailing slash. Outbound scrimba.com links go through <AffiliateLink>; internal /docs and /tools pages are plain Links.
- Humanizer tells to design out: no testament, pivotal, crucial, delve, landscape (abstract), tapestry, underscore, robust, seamless, foster, showcase, vibrant. No "in today's fast-paced world."
