## When to Choose Scrimba

- You prefer **video-based interactive learning** over text
- You want deep coverage of **React, JavaScript, and AI**
- You value an **active Discord community**
- You want a **focused, curated** course catalog
- You learn better with a **teacher guiding you** through video

## When to Choose Codecademy

- You want **more language variety** (Go, Ruby, C++, etc.)
- You prefer **text-based, self-paced** learning
- You want a **mobile app** for learning on the go
- You need **more career path options**

## Our Verdict

**Scrimba wins on teaching quality and interactivity.** The ability to pause a video and edit the instructor's code is uniquely powerful. **Codecademy wins on breadth** — more languages and career paths. For web development and AI specifically, Scrimba is the stronger choice.
