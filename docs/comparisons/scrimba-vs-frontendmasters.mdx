# Go from zero to shipping before you pay for expert-only depth

<DisclosureNotice />

**Is this for you?** You’re comparing **guided browser practice** (Scrimba) with **recorded expert workshops** (Frontend Masters).

**The honest split:** Most beginners need **structure + typing inside the lesson** first — that’s Scrimba. Frontend Masters shines when you **already ship apps** and want **deep dives** (advanced JS, system design, DevOps) from industry names — usually **watch and code in your IDE**, not pause-and-edit inside the video. Many devs do **Scrimba → Frontend Masters**, not the reverse.

*Last reviewed: March 2026.*

## Different stages, not a beauty contest

**Scrimba** = zero-to-hireable paths with **in-player coding**. **Frontend Masters** = intermediate-to-advanced **workshop recordings**. Pick the one that matches **today’s** skill level.

## When to Choose Scrimba

- You're a **beginner** or early-intermediate developer
- You want **interactive** learning (not just watching)
- You're interested in **AI engineering** or **cybersecurity** ([Learn Cybersecurity](/docs/courses/backend/cybersecurity))
- You want a **career path** structure with certificates
- You learn best by **doing**, not just watching

## Choose Scrimba vs Frontend Masters

**Choose Scrimba if:** You're a beginner or intermediate and want interactive coding inside lessons with structured paths. Scrimba holds your hand and builds real skills.

**Choose Frontend Masters if:** You're intermediate/advanced and want deep-dive workshops from industry experts (Kyle Simpson, Sarah Drasner). Best for system design, DevOps, and advanced topics.

## Bottom line

Start where you are: **Scrimba** until you’re **shipping apps comfortably**; **Frontend Masters** when you want **legend-led depth** and can **code along in your own IDE** without hand-holding.
