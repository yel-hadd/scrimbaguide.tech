## When to Choose Scrimba

- You're a **beginner** or early-intermediate developer
- You want **interactive** learning (not just watching)
- You're interested in **AI engineering** courses
- You want a **career path** structure with certificates
- You learn best by **doing**, not just watching

## When to Choose Frontend Masters

- You're an **intermediate or advanced** developer
- You want to learn from **industry experts** (Kyle Simpson, Sarah Drasner, etc.)
- You prefer **workshop-style** deep dives
- You're interested in **system design, DevOps, or advanced topics**
