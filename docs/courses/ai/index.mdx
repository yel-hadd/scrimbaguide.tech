## Stuck watching AI tutorials that never ship?

Passive videos and blog chains leave you guessing when a header, stream, or tool schema breaks. **Scrimba scrims** let you **pause, edit the instructor’s code, and rerun** in the browser—closer to how you will debug APIs and UIs at work than copying snippets into a blank repo.

## Ship AI features in JS without drowning in setup

Every course here stays in **JavaScript/TypeScript** so you can add models and tools to the stack you already use. Free entry points ([Claude](/docs/courses/ai/claude-ai), [Mistral](/docs/courses/ai/mistral-ai), [serverless agents with Langbase](/docs/courses/ai/serverless-ai-agents-with-langbase)) sit next to **Pro** depth: **agents, RAG, LangChain.js, MCP, Vercel AI SDK, context engineering**, and **Cloudflare deployment**. The [AI Engineer Path](/docs/paths/ai-engineer-path) orders the stack so you are not guessing what to take next.

**We are independent:** we summarize Scrimba honestly—**Pro costs money**, free tiers rotate, and **no course replaces** portfolio work and interviews. Use [Practice AI Engineering](/docs/practice/practice-ai-engineering) between modules when you want reps without a greenfield repo.
