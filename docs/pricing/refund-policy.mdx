# Scrimba Refund Policy

<DisclosureNotice />

Scrimba offers refunds under specific conditions. Here's what you need to know before subscribing — and what to do if you want your money back.

## Refund Rules

- **Full refunds** are available if requested **within 7 days** of your initial purchase
- **One refund per account** — you can only request a refund once
- **No pro-rated refunds** — if you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access until the end of the billing period but won't receive a partial refund
- **No refunds on renewals** — automatic renewal charges are not eligible for refunds
- **Refunds are based on the amount received** — any bank or exchange fees are excluded

## How to Request a Refund

1. [Submit a refund request](https://form.typeform.com/to/PJFkZVF7) through Scrimba's official form
2. If you need additional assistance, email **help@scrimba.com**
3. Refunds are typically processed within a few business days

[Scrimba Help Centre — How do I get a refund?](https://scrimba.helpscoutdocs.com/article/6-how-do-i-get-a-refund)

## What Happens After Cancellation?

- **Your progress is saved** — even after canceling, your course progress remains on your account
- **Free courses stay accessible** — you can still access all Community-labeled courses
- **Pro courses become locked** — you'll need to re-subscribe to access Pro content
- **Discord access is revoked** — you lose access to private Pro channels

## Tips Before Subscribing

1. **Try the free courses first** — Scrimba has ~{freeCount} free courses including [Learn React](/docs/courses/react/) and [Learn JavaScript](/docs/courses/javascript/)
2. **Check the [course catalog](/docs/courses/)** — make sure Scrimba covers what you want to learn
3. **Choose annual if committed** — better value and you can still cancel within 7 days for a full refund
