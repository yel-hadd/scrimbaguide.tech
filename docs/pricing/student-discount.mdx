# Scrimba student savings (email, GitHub, hardship)

<DisclosureNotice />

**Quick answer:** Three honest levers: (**1**) email **help@scrimba.com** from a **school address** for a student rate, (**2**) redeem **one free Pro month** through the **GitHub Student Developer Pack**, (**3**) write support if you are in **real hardship**. There is **no universal public coupon**—Scrimba applies discounts after verification.

*Last reviewed: March 2026. Discount terms may change — contact Scrimba or check their help centre for current policies.*

## Who should read this

Students with a **.edu** (or equivalent) inbox, anyone **GitHub Education** already approved, or learners who need a **human** answer when price is the blocker.

## Option 1: Academic Email Discount

If you have a valid `.edu` or other academic email address, you can request a student discount directly from Scrimba.

**How to apply:**
1. Email **help@scrimba.com** from your academic email address
2. Request the student discount
3. The Scrimba team will process your request and apply the discount to your account

## Option 2: GitHub Student Developer Pack

The <AffiliateLink href="https://scrimba.com/github-education?via=u42d4986">GitHub Student Developer Pack</AffiliateLink> includes **1 free month of Scrimba Pro** for eligible students.

**How to redeem:**
1. Apply for the [GitHub Student Developer Pack](https://education.github.com/pack)
2. Once approved, visit the <AffiliateLink href="https://scrimba.com/github-education?via=u42d4986">Scrimba GitHub Education page</AffiliateLink> and log in with GitHub
3. In the checkout modal, look for the "GitHub Student 30-Day Trial" message and the yellow **UPGRADE** button to activate your free month

[Scrimba Help Centre — How do I get the Student Github free access?](https://scrimba.helpscoutdocs.com/article/46-how-do-i-access-the-student-github-free-access)

## Option 3: Scholarships

Scrimba does not currently offer a formal scholarship program, but they encourage reaching out to **help@scrimba.com** if you're in a financial hardship situation.

## Choose Your Discount

**Choose the academic email discount if:** You have a valid .edu or academic email and want an ongoing reduced rate.

**Choose the GitHub Student Pack if:** You're verified by GitHub and want 1 free month to try Pro before committing.

**Reach out about hardship if:** You don't qualify for the above but need financial assistance — Scrimba encourages contacting help@scrimba.com.

## What You Get as a Student

With the student discount or GitHub pack, you get the same benefits as a regular Pro subscriber:

- All courses and 4 career paths
- Unlimited interactive challenges
- Private Discord community access
- Certificates of completion
