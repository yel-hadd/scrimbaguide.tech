## Choose Pro vs Stay Free

**Choose Pro if:** You want structured career paths, unlimited challenges, Discord access, or certificates for your resume — and you plan to study consistently for 3+ months.

**Stay Free if:** You're testing the format, only need React/JavaScript basics, or are supplementing another resource. You can always upgrade later.
