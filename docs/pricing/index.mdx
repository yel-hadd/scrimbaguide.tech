# Scrimba Pricing Guide 2026

Scrimba offers a free tier and a paid Pro subscription. Here's everything you need to know about the costs, what's included, and how to save.

## Pricing Plans at a Glance

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | ~20 courses, limited challenges (10 total), no Discord |
| **Pro Monthly** | Per-month billing | All 87+ courses, unlimited challenges, private Discord, certificates |
| **Pro Annual** | Annual billing (best value) | Same as monthly but with significant annual savings |

> Prices may change — check the <AffiliateLink href="https://scrimba.com/home?pricing">official Scrimba pricing page</AffiliateLink> for current rates.

## What's Included in Scrimba Pro?

- **Full access** to all current and future premium courses
- **4 career paths**: [Frontend](/docs/paths/frontend-developer-path) (81.6 hrs), [Fullstack](/docs/paths/fullstack-developer-path) (108.4 hrs), [Backend](/docs/paths/backend-developer-path) (30.1 hrs), [AI Engineer](/docs/paths/ai-engineer-path) (11.4 hrs)
- **Unlimited interactive challenges**
- **Private Discord community** with mentors and industry professionals
- **Certificates of completion** for career paths
- **New courses added regularly** — see the [Scrimba roadmap](/blog/scrimba-roadmap-whats-coming)
