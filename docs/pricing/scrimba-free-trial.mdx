## Does Scrimba have a free trial?

No, not in the usual SaaS sense. There is no countdown that hands you full Pro for a week and then locks you out. People search for a "Scrimba free trial" because they want to test the product before paying, and that instinct is right. You just reach it through a different door. In fact you can learn on Scrimba for months without paying a cent, because the free account is permanent, not a teaser.

### 1. The free account (the real "free Scrimba")

This is the closest thing to an unlimited free trial, because it does not expire. Create a free account and you get {freeCountLabel} full courses, up to 10 interactive coding challenges, weekly live events, and the community. No credit card, no clock. You can finish entire courses like Learn JavaScript or Learn React on the free tier and never pay.

The limits are honest ones: career paths, unlimited challenges, the private Discord, and path certificates sit behind Pro. If you want the full picture of where free ends, read [Pro vs Free](/docs/pricing/pro-vs-free) and [Is Scrimba free?](/docs/how-it-works/is-scrimba-free).

### 3. The 7-day guarantee (your real risk-free Pro trial)

This is the route people actually mean when they search for a free trial of Pro. Scrimba backs a first Pro purchase with a 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. That means you can subscribe, unlock every course, path, and challenge, work through the parts you care about for a week, and request a full refund if it does not fit how you learn. It is a trial in everything but name, and you keep more control than a typical auto-converting trial because you decide whether to stay.

Two honest caveats: you pay up front and wait for the refund, and the guarantee is meant for genuine first-time evaluation, not serial refunding. The mechanics, including how to request it, are in the [refund policy](/docs/pricing/refund-policy).

### 4. Students: one free month of Pro via GitHub

If you are a verified student, this is the closest thing to a true free trial of Pro. Scrimba is part of the [GitHub Student Developer Pack](https://education.github.com/pack), which includes one free month of Scrimba Pro.

1. Apply for the [GitHub Student Developer Pack](https://education.github.com/pack). You usually need a school email or a photo of your enrollment ID. GitHub does the verifying.
2. Once approved, open the <AffiliateLink href="https://scrimba.com/github-education?via=u42d4986">Scrimba GitHub Education page</AffiliateLink> and sign in with GitHub.
3. In the checkout modal, find the GitHub Student 30-day offer and click upgrade to activate your free Pro month.

The free month is one-time, not recurring, so you fall back to free when it ends unless you subscribe. Full detail and the academic-email discount are on the [student discount](/docs/pricing/student-discount) page.

## What about coupon codes?

There is no universal public coupon for Scrimba. If a coupon site shows you "SCRIMBA50," treat it as fiction unless it lives on Scrimba's own domain. The two durable savings are the student paths above and the standing partner discount our links apply at checkout. We track any live, time-boxed promotions in the [2026 discount codes guide](/blog/scrimba-discount-codes-2026), and seasonal sales tend to cluster around Black Friday and New Year, not random codes.
