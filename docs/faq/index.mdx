# Scrimba FAQ

*Last reviewed: May 28, 2026.*

**Is Scrimba legit, and is it worth it for beginners?** Yes, Scrimba is a legitimate coding school, not a scam. It has run since 2017, holds a "Great" rating on [Trustpilot](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/scrimba.com) and a strong reviewer score on [Class Central](https://www.classcentral.com/provider/scrimba), and its instructors are public, named people. For beginners it works best as a structured, project-based alternative to scattered YouTube tutorials. The honest catch is that it is self-paced, so outcomes still depend on your consistency. Try the [free courses](/docs/pricing/pro-vs-free/) before paying for Pro.

**Is this page for you?** You want to know if Scrimba fits your goal, what Pro actually unlocks, and what happens to your progress if you cancel, without digging through scattered help articles.

**Quick picture:** Scrimba teaches in **interactive scrims** (code inside the lesson). **Free** gets you substantial courses and a capped number of challenges; **Pro** unlocks **all courses**, **career paths**, **private Discord**, **unlimited challenges**, and **path certificates**. Cancel anytime; your progress stays on your account.
