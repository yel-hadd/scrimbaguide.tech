# Scrimba FAQ

**Is this page for you?** You want to know if Scrimba fits your goal, what Pro actually unlocks, and what happens to your progress if you cancel, without digging through scattered help articles.

**Quick picture:** Scrimba teaches in **interactive scrims** (code inside the lesson). **Free** gets you substantial courses and a capped number of challenges; **Pro** unlocks **all courses**, **career paths**, **private Discord**, **unlimited challenges**, and **path certificates**. Cancel anytime; your progress stays on your account.
