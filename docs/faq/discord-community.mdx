# Scrimba Discord Community FAQ

**Short answer:** The private Discord is Pro-only. Link your Scrimba account from the dashboard, ask in the channel matching your course or topic, and include what you already tried. 55,000+ members, peak-hour responses in minutes.

You're on Pro (or considering it) and want fast code help, events, or career channels, not another dead forum.

## Who this is for

Pro subscribers (or prospects) sorting invites, linking errors, and where to post. Full feature tour on the [community and events guide](/docs/faq/community-and-events).
