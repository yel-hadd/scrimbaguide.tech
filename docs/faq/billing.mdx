# Scrimba Billing & Payments FAQ

**Quick Answer:** Pro is subscription-only (monthly or annual). Refunds within 7 days of first purchase. Student discounts and GitHub Pack available. Cancel anytime; progress is saved.

*Last reviewed: March 2026.*

## Who This Is For

Subscribers or potential subscribers with billing questions: pricing, refunds, payment methods, student discounts, or subscription management.

## Fast Navigation

- **Need current plan pricing?** Check [Scrimba Pricing](/docs/pricing/) first, then use the FAQs below for edge cases.
- **Looking for discounts?** See [Student Discount](/docs/pricing/student-discount) and [Discount Codes & Deals](/blog/scrimba-discount-codes-2026).
- **Unsure whether to upgrade?** Compare [Pro vs Free](/docs/pricing/pro-vs-free).
