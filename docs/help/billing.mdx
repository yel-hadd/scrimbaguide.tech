# Scrimba Billing & Payments

**Short answer:** Pro is subscription-only (monthly or annual, no one-off course purchases). Refunds are honored within 7 days of first purchase. Student discounts and the GitHub Student Pack are available. Cancel anytime from account settings; your progress stays on your account.

For the full plan breakdown, see [Pro vs Free](/docs/pricing/pro-vs-free). For current numbers, [scrimba.com/our-pricing](https://scrimba.com/our-pricing) is the source of truth.

## Who This Is For

Subscribers or potential subscribers with billing questions: pricing, refunds, payment methods, student discounts, or subscription management.

## How Scrimba billing works in one minute

There is only one paid product: Pro. There is no per-course purchase and no lifetime buyout, so every billing question reduces to how you pay for Pro and how you stop.

- **Two billing cycles.** Monthly or annual. Annual works out to roughly half the per-month rate, so if you will study for three months or more, annual is the cheaper unit. Weigh it on the [Pro vs Free comparison](/docs/pricing/pro-vs-free).
- **Region-aware pricing.** Scrimba applies purchasing-power parity automatically, so the price you see can be lower than the headline rate depending on your country. No coupon needed.
- **One renewal switch.** A subscription renews until you cancel. Cancelling stops the next charge but keeps your access to the end of the period; it does not refund the current term. The two actions are different, and the [refund policy](/docs/pricing/refund-policy) lays out which one returns money.
- **Your progress is yours.** Cancel, lapse, and resubscribe months later, and your completed lessons are still on your account.

If you only remember one thing: try the [free tier](/docs/pricing/pro-vs-free) first, pick annual once you are confident, and you keep the 7-day refund window on that first charge either way.
